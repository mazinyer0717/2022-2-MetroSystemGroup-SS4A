FAQ

¿Is MetroSystem free?

MetroSystem is a completely free application, you should not pay for any functionality.

¿What platforms are they available on?

It is currently available for Android 8 or higher and iOS.

¿I can recharge my card balance anywhere and anytime?

Yes, you can recharge at any time and wherever you are. The only thing necessary is an internet connection to be able to carry out the balance transfer or balance top-up.

¿If I want to transfer the balance of my card to another person, what information do I need?

The only thing you need to be able to transfer the balance of your card to another card is the ID, then the transfer will be made instantly by following the transfer steps.

¿What does the MetroSystem route guide show?

It shows the bus that you must board with the correct route, the time and distance that is missing for you to reach your destination and finally the alternate routes that you can take once you have finished the trip with the Metropolitano service..
